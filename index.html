Questions

Honest answers.

Is this only for Madinah, or do you cover other cities?

Al-Nomaad is built specifically for Madinah. It is where the consultancy is based, which is what makes on-ground services possible. Consultations can cover other cities and countries for context, but property work and practical services are Madinah-specific.

Not sure which package is right for me.

Start with a Discovery Call or the free 20-minute planning session. You will be told honestly which package — or individual service — fits where you are. If none of them are right yet, that will be said too.

What does the property inspection cover?

40+ checkpoints, photos included, written report delivered within 24–48 hours. All backed by the 90-day confidence guarantee — full refund and next report free if a major issue is missed.

I'm a sister moving alone. Is Al-Nomaad set up for that?

Yes. The service is built for Muslims in all circumstances, including sisters. Community connections include families and sisters already settled in Madinah. Safety and suitability are part of every neighbourhood and property assessment.

Can I get help with the Iqama without Tier Three?

Iqama facilitation is part of Tier Three. If you are not yet ready for the full package, the Iqama route can be discussed in a Madinah Readiness Briefing or any consultation session.

How quickly are property reports delivered?

Property and Area Inspection: within 24 hours of the visit. Area-only: within 48 hours. Reach out via Instagram @alnomaad for property-specific enquiries and current availability.

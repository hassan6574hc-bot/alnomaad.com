Questions

Honest answers.

Is this only for Madinah, or do you cover other cities?

Al-Nomaad is built specifically for Madinah. It is where the consultancy is permanently based, which is what makes on-ground services possible. Consultations can cover other cities for context, but property work and practical services are Madinah-specific.

Not sure which package is right for me.

Start with the Hijrah Discovery Call (£45). You will get an honest recommendation on which package — or individual service — fits your situation. If none of them are right yet, that will be said too.

What does the property inspection cover?

40+ checkpoints, photos included, written report delivered within 48 hours of the visit. The inspection covers structural condition, proximity to the Haram, noise levels, landlord assessment, and everything needed to make a confident decision remotely.

I'm a sister moving alone. Is Al-Nomaad set up for that?

Al-Nomaad works with sisters who are travelling with a mahram. Community connections include families and sisters already settled in Madinah, and safety is factored into every neighbourhood and property assessment.

Can I get Iqama help without Tier Three?

Iqama facilitation is part of Tier Three. The Iqama route can be discussed in any consultation session — including the Hijrah Discovery Call — so you understand the process before committing to a package.

How quickly are property reports delivered?

Area inspection only: within 24 hours. Full property and area inspection: within 48 hours. To request a report, book a consultation and select Property Inspection as your service.

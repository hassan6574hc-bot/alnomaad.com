Questions

Honest answers.

Is this only for Madinah, or do you cover other cities?

Al-Nomaad is built specifically for Madinah. It is where the consultancy is permanently based, which is what makes on-ground services possible. Consultations can cover other cities and countries for context, but property work and practical services are Madinah-specific.

Not sure which package is right for me.

Start with the 30-minute clarity call or the Hijrah Discovery Call. You will get an honest recommendation on which package — or individual service — fits. If none of them are right yet, that will be said too.

What does the property inspection cover?

40+ checkpoints, photos included, written report delivered within 48 hours of the visit.

I'm a sister moving alone. Is Al-Nomaad set up for that?

Al-Nomaad works with sisters who are travelling with a mahram. If that is your situation, the full service applies — community connections include families and sisters already settled in Madinah, and safety is factored into every neighbourhood and property assessment.

Can I get help with the Iqama without Tier Three?

Iqama facilitation is part of Tier Three. If you are not yet ready for the full package, the Iqama route can be discussed in a Madinah Readiness Briefing or any consultation session.

How quickly are property reports delivered?

Area Inspection only: within 24 hours. Property and Area Inspection: within 48 hours. To request a report, book a consultation and select Property Inspection as your service.
